▸ Why now

Technology stopped being the differentiator. Operating on it is.

AI made building software cheap — everyone has the same tools now. What separates winners is how deeply AI actually runs the work: the proposals, the approvals, the reconciliations, the reports. Companies carrying legacy systems and manual processes will feel it first. Almost nobody is building companies future-proofed for this. That's the gap we fill.

Where we come in

Inside the systems you already run — ERP, spreadsheets, messaging — through secure, permissioned access, the way open banking connects apps to accounts. For sensitive work, the AI models run on your own infrastructure: your data never leaves your walls.

How we measure

Every engagement carries a simple score from 0 to 100 — how much of the work runs agentically. It climbs as agents take on more, and it keeps both sides honest about progress. A measure, not a promise.
